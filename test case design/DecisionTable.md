# Decision Table Scenario

## User Story
Customers can sign up for loans that have interest rates determined by a variety of factors

### Decision Table for Interest Charged

|Income|Loan Period|Preferred Customer|Interest Charged|
|------|-----------|------------------|----------------|
|30k| 10 | no | 10%|
|30k| 5 | no | 15% |
|30k| 1 | no | 25% |
|30k| 10 | yes |8%|
|etc.|etc.|etc.|etc.|

NOTE: to get the most benefit from your decision table make sure you know what all your
potential categories of data are so you do not overfill your decision table. The goal of putting
all your data into a decision table is to create an approachable organization of your test data
and specify the expected results of that data being used in your tests
